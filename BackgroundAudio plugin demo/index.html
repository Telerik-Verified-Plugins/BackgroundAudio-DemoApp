BackgroundAudio Plugin Demo

Allows your app to keep on playing audio when it's in the background. There is no additional configuration or Javascript call required to trigger the plugin.

Only iOS is supported.

Custom plugins don't work in the simulator and the AppBuilder companion app, so inside Telerik AppBuilder choose Run - Build - iOS - App package.

Play an audio stream

After clicking 'Play' press the home button of your device - the audio should keep on playing.
